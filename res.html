First Responders

I get requests from public safety agencies within the State of Colorado for pictures of their community events, vehicles, and other things for their public relations team and to also have photos to hang up in their stations.

I've done photography work for:

  • Brighton Fire Rescue District
  • Florissant Fire Protection District (southern Colorado)
  • North Metro Fire Rescue (Broomfield)
  • Colorado State Patrol
  • Windsor-Severance Fire Rescue
  • & many more!
